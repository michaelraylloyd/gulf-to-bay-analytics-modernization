# Dashboard Catalog

This catalog provides an overview of all dashboards built on the Semantic Model. It defines each dashboard’s purpose, intended audience, key metrics, and analytical scope. The catalog ensures that users can quickly understand which dashboard to use for which business question.

---

## Executive Summary Dashboard

**Purpose:**  
Provide leadership with a high‑level view of organizational performance.

**Audience:**  
Executives, directors, senior managers.

**Key Metrics:**  
- Total Revenue  
- Gross Margin  
- Customer Count  
- Month‑over‑Month Growth  
- Top‑Level KPIs  

**Scope:**  
Enterprise‑wide performance with drill‑downs into major business areas.

---

## Sales Performance Dashboard

**Purpose:**  
Analyze sales trends, product performance, and customer purchasing behavior.

**Audience:**  
Sales leadership, sales analysts, revenue teams.

**Key Metrics:**  
- Total Revenue  
- Quantity Sold  
- Average Order Value  
- Revenue by Product  
- Revenue by Region  

**Scope:**  
Transaction‑level insights with drill‑downs into product, customer, and region.

---

## Customer Insights Dashboard

**Purpose:**  
Understand customer behavior, segmentation, and retention patterns.

**Audience:**  
Marketing teams, customer success, analytics teams.

**Key Metrics:**  
- Customer Count  
- New vs. Repeat Customers  
- Revenue per Customer  
- Customer Segments  
- Lifetime Value Indicators  

**Scope:**  
Customer‑centric analytics with SCD2‑aware historical views.

---

## Product Performance Dashboard

**Purpose:**  
Evaluate product profitability, category performance, and SKU‑level trends.

**Audience:**  
Product managers, merchandising teams, finance analysts.

**Key Metrics:**  
- Product Revenue Contribution  
- Quantity Sold by Product  
- Gross Margin by Product  
- Category and Subtype Trends  

**Scope:**  
Product‑level analytics with drill paths into category and subtype.

---

## Operational Monitoring Dashboard

**Purpose:**  
Provide near‑real‑time visibility into operational metrics.

**Audience:**  
Operations teams, support teams, real‑time monitoring groups.

**Key Metrics:**  
- Event Volume  
- Real‑Time KPIs  
- Micro‑Batch Aggregates  
- Streaming Alerts  

**Scope:**  
Low‑latency insights powered by real‑time and incremental processing.

---

## Regional Performance Dashboard

**Purpose:**  
Analyze performance across geographic regions.

**Audience:**  
Regional managers, field teams, executive leadership.

**Key Metrics:**  
- Revenue by Region  
- Quantity Sold by Region  
- Regional Growth Trends  
- Customer Distribution  

**Scope:**  
Geography‑based analytics with drill‑downs into state, city, and segment.

---

## Finance & Profitability Dashboard

**Purpose:**  
Provide financial insights into revenue, cost, and margin performance.

**Audience:**  
Finance teams, FP&A, executive leadership.

**Key Metrics:**  
- Gross Margin  
- Gross Margin Percentage  
- Revenue vs. Cost Trends  
- Profitability by Product  

**Scope:**  
Financial analytics aligned with curated Gold‑layer fact models.

---

## Summary

This catalog provides a structured overview of all dashboards built on the Semantic Model. It ensures that users can quickly identify the right dashboard for their needs and understand the analytical purpose behind each reporting experience.
